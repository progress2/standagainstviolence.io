About Us!

We are part of a program called GirlsWhoCode, and we are challenged to create a website that covers an issue we value important. We made StandAgainstDomesticViolence in order to spread awareness of domestic abuse and offer resources for those who need. StandAgainstDomesticViolence believes they can provide victims with help and spread awareness to prevent this from occurring in the future.
